About Us

Pyrotechnic Apps was founded in in 2013 to deliver mobile app design and build projects as an end-to-end service.

Our Focus

We focus on producing mobile solutions for small to medium-sized businesses (SMEs), providing a full service right from the inital idea to the completed app and back end web services.

Our apps range from the larger transaction processing to smaller, simple apps with one specific targeted purpose.

We can manage the whole project, bring in the right resources and transform your idea into a completed app, leaving you free to focus on your business.
