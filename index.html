Pyrotechnic Apps was setup in in 2013 to deliver mobile app design and build projects as an end-to-end service.

Our Focus

We focus on for mobile apps for small to medium business – to meet the self-service demand and create new and disruptive business models and markets.

Many of these apps are about transaction processing rather than smaller, brand intensive apps.

Our Apps

Our Apps

Voogo

A coupon app focusing on live, immediately available deals. This app allows vendors to create and distribute promotional coupons on the fly to manage stock and resource levels in line with immediate demand. e.g. A sandwich shop that has too many sandwiches at 4pm or a hair salon that a day with large gaps in its booking schedule.

Ventus AV

A private Jet Booking app – think of uber for jets. This app has been developed to Proof of Concept stage, and is demonstrable and ready to run a pilot. It was developed for a new start-up business that is currently seeking a partner to launch the business.

Blocks

A resource booking and repair app for a residential housing complex – to distribute news, log repairs needed, allocate, track and complete them, and for shared facilities to be booked, such as parking.

Ventus AV

VentusAV is a revolutionary new platform in the Private Aviation Charter Market that uses cutting-edge technology to bypass the traditional broker and connect the Customer and Operator directly

Ventus wanted a premium branded iPhone app and equivalent website that would be simple to use. take the user right through from requesting a flight to the completed booking. In addition, they wanted a web portalto allow the operators to respond to the bespoke flight requests from customers.

Timescales formed a critical part of this project, in order that the app be delivered in good time for the Ventus team to take the app to an air show to attract investors.

Pyrotechnic Apps worked with VentusAV Ltd to develop a new app for the Private Aviation Charter Market. We worked with the team at Ventus from the initial concept right through to having the product ready to show to investors.
