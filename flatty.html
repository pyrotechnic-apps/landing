An exciting new App designed to help management of blocks of flats and housing complexes.

Pyrotechnic Apps developed Flatty as one of its own projects. It is an app for residents and managers of a housing complex, to distribute news and report necessary repairs and track these through allocation to completion. It also facilitates the booking of shared facilities such as parking.





Easily release news, manage jobs, parking and other bookings all in one easy to use app backed with a cloud based service so there is nothing to install.
